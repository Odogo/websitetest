About Odogo

I started with Java to make spigot plugins for the servers I worked on or for my own personal use to make minigames or other things. Not until I was 14, I started to learn C# for Unity. I've since then been working on my own projects and currently working on a few games that might come out later. I created my twitch account, but didn't really do anything with it until the summer of 2020, when I started to actually stream.
